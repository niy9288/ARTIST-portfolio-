About the Artist

Discover the story behind the art and the inspiration that drives each brushstroke.

Yves RUGAMBA is a contemporary artist whose work explores the profound relationship between light, shadow, and human emotion. Born and now based in Kigali, his artistic journey began with a fascination for the way light transforms ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences.

His paintings, whether in oil or watercolor, capture the delicate balance between the seen and the felt. Each piece is a meditation on the human condition, rendered through a masterful understanding of color theory and composition. Yves's work has been described as "emotionally resonant" and "visually arresting" by critics and collectors alike.

When not in his studio overlooking the Mediterranean, Yves can be found wandering the streets of Kigali, camera in hand, seeking those fleeting moments of beauty that inspire his next creation. His work has been exhibited in galleries across Europe and is held in private collections worldwide.

Artist Statement

"I believe that art has the power to connect us to our deepest emotions and to each other. Through my work, I strive to capture not just what I see, but what I feel in those quiet moments of observation. Each painting is a conversation between light and shadow, between the artist and the viewer, between the past and the present."
